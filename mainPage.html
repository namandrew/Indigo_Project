Sensory Mini-Games!

In this website, you will find four mini-games: a Color, Visual, Hearing, and the classic Flappy Bird. You start with once and once you finish that game, you move onto another mini-game. If you are able to finish all four, you win!

Color Search

Try and find the colored square that differs from the rest.

Vision Test

Determine how good your vision is with a simple eye test.

Hearing Test

Listen to audio recordings and try to find out what you just heard.

Flappy Bird

Remember that game that took the world by storm a couple years ago? Well, it's back, and it's here for you to play.
